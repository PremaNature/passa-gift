The mechanism

A gift that
remembers where it came from.

Every product in the passa ecosystem carries a unique QR code — not just a link to a website, but a living chain page that grows with each passing.

When someone receives a passa product, they discover the story of every person who gifted it before them. The chain becomes a cultural artefact. A timeline of human generosity.

For brands, this means organic reach measured not in impressions, but in meaningful human connections — each node a new customer, a new ambassador, a new story attributed to your product.

No incentives. No rewards. No cashback. Pure voluntary continuation — because the story itself is worth passing on.

Why passa works

01

Living brand equity

Every chain node is a new customer discovery — organic, authentic, attributed directly to your product's journey through the world.

02

No-incentive growth

Passa's continuation model relies on intrinsic motivation — story, status and meaning — making your brand associated with genuine human generosity, not cashback mechanics.

03

Real-time intelligence

Brand dashboards show geographic spread, chain depth, drop-off analysis and node demographics — turning gifting into your most insightful marketing channel.
